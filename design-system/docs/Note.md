---
category: Feedback
---

Inline callout box for statuses, hosted-instance notices, and errors. Indigo-tinted info by default; `variant="error"` is red. Preserves line breaks (`white-space: pre-wrap`).

```jsx
<Note>Brief written — 30 shots planned. Review the shot list below.</Note>
<Note variant="error">Farm stopped: budget cap reached at $300.</Note>
```
