Collapsed-by-default `<details>` holding a scrollable monospace log — pipeline noise stays out of the way until it's wanted.

```jsx
<LogDisclosure summary="Pipeline log">
{`10:41 brief written (30 shots)
10:44 rendering 1–6 …
10:52 shot 2 QC 4.2 — queued for retake`}
</LogDisclosure>
```
